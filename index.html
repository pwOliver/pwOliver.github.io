About Me

Who Am I?

Welcome to my portfolio website. I am Yulin Qian, currently a graduate student at Shanghai University. My interests lie at the intersection of technical art, computer graphics, procedural content generation, and AI-assisted digital asset creation.

With a cross-disciplinary background in digital media and computing, I focus on combining visual aesthetics with technical implementation. I am particularly interested in stylized rendering, shader development, real-time graphics, and tool or pipeline design that can improve the efficiency and quality of content production.

My long-term goal is to grow into a technical artist who can bridge art and engineering, and contribute to game development through rendering, procedural workflows, and intelligent content creation tools.
